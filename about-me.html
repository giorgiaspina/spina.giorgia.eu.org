Background

I come from a background in the culinary industry, where I worked for several years and had the opportunity to manage a professional kitchen. This experience has equipped me with strong soft skills such as problem-solving under pressure, team coordination, time management, and attention to detail — all essential in data engineering as well.

Driven by a passion for technology and structured thinking, I decided to shift my career path and completed a specialized training course with Generation Italy as a Junior Data Engineer. This experience allowed me to acquire foundational skills in data pipelines, SQL, Python, NoSQL databases, and data visualization (Power BI), laying the groundwork for my future in tech.

Career Aspirations

I aspire to grow as a Data Engineer by building solid experience in designing and managing data pipelines, starting from real-world projects and cloud-based tools. My goal is to deepen my skills in handling large-scale datasets and gradually move toward integrating data engineering with areas like machine learning and advanced analytics. I'm especially motivated to work in dynamic environments where I can learn from experienced professionals and contribute to impactful data solutions.
